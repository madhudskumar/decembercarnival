CityBomb

Bombing the City

Run! Run! Run!
The bomb has dropped in your city.
It is a fun game. A bunch of participants will move in a circle to the tune of some musical sound. When the music stops, each participant will have to choose a corner containing the choice of city names. Out of the four city names, one will be selected randomly and participants who belong to the randomly selected city shall be bombed (eliminated) and declared out of the game. Winner and runner-up shall emerge.

What to do?
Just run when the music is turned on.

Where will it happen?
