PeepulTrail

Finders keepers

Do you think you know your office premises well? We challenge your confidence.
Peepul Trail is a self-guided treasure hunt experience on your mobile. A quest in 30.5 sq. m premise.
Our quests are a series of clues. Simple or tough is just a state of mind and some willingness for old-fashioned asking around.
Solve the clues, find the place and validate. Soak the zeal to be the first to reach the final destination. This self-guided experience means.Everyone is a winner.
The game routes are specially curated to take to nook and corner of the office premise.
Come as a group and challenge your opponents.

Team Size
The team should consist of 4 members.

What you need ?
Smartphone per team, water, willingness to search and be challenged.
