MindGame

Test your Recollection

This consists of two activities where your brain is put to work.

Mind your mind
Memorize and Recollect!!!
This game tests your memory where a picture with a variety of items will be shown. Memorize as many items as you can. The picture will be shown for 30sec. Later, recollect the name of the items. The person with highest item names will emerge as the winner.

Connect
Find the connection.
This is a game where your wit and creativeness comes handy. Collection of few pictures will be presented to the participants.They have to connect and find out the link between them and come up with a single word or phrase from those set of pictures.
