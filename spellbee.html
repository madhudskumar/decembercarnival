Are you a spelling pro?
Be ready to test your vocabulary.
A Spell bee is a fun game in which contestants are asked to spell a broad selection of words, usually with a varying degree of difficulty.
The pair which succeeds in spelling more words correctly shall emerge as a winner.

Rules:
1. Associates shall participate in pairs.
2. Usage of mobile phones is prohibited.

Rounds:
1. Pick and Spell
2. Jumbled Words
3. Fill in the missing letters
4. Find wrong spelled words.

What do you need?
A pen and a good vocabulary

Where will it be?
One of the auditoriums

Time required:
90 minutes for an expected count of 20-25 teams
